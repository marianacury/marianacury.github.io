Intro

Not just a pretty face.

From Brazil but living in California with a 2-year layover in the Cayman Islands. Designer for 10 years now. Started working with graphic design in print - newspapers back home specifically, then moved to web design and finally jumped ship to mobile design. There's just something so rewarding about dealing with the small screen, allowing us to craft intimate experiences with a real focus on our main advocate - the user. For the past few years I've been mainly working on building apps (iOS and Android), which I can honestly say that's where my passion lives.

I love Frank Sinatra, David Bowie, vintage furniture, french bulldogs, coffee and old Super Nintendo games. The order of the factors does not change the person.
